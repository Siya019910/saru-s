Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your visit. If your question isn't here, just ask us on WhatsApp!

✦   General

How do I book an appointment? +

Simply WhatsApp or call us on 066 277 3338 to book your slot. We recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred date and time.

Please contact us directly on 066 277 3338 for current operating hours, as times may vary. We try to accommodate flexible schedules wherever possible.

Do I need to make a deposit? +

For training classes and some services a small deposit may be required to confirm your booking. Please enquire via WhatsApp for the current deposit policy.

✦   Services
What nail services do you offer? +

We offer classic manicures, acrylic extensions, nail art, French tips, gel overlay, builder gel, gel polish, and nail repairs. Whether simple or elaborate, we have you covered.

Do you do wig installations for all hair types? +

Yes! We install lace front wigs, full cap wigs, and frontal units. We work with all hair types and textures to ensure a secure, natural-looking finish.

Can I bring my own lashes or wig? +

Absolutely! You are welcome to bring your own products. Just let us know in advance so we can ensure we have everything prepared for your appointment.

Do you offer make-up for special events like weddings? +

Yes! We offer make-up for all occasions — weddings, matric dances, graduations, birthday parties, and more. Book early for special occasions to avoid disappointment.

How long does gel overlay last? +

Gel overlay typically lasts 2–4 weeks depending on your nail growth and how well you care for your nails. We recommend infills every 2–3 weeks for the best results.

✦   Beauty Training
What training courses do you offer? +

We offer professional training in nails, wig installation, make-up artistry, lash application, and gel overlay. Each course is hands-on and practical, taught by Saru herself.

Is a training kit included in the course? +

Yes! A full professional training kit is provided in every class. You will leave with everything you need to start practising your new skills right away — no extra cost for your kit.

Do I need prior experience to join a training class? +

No prior experience is needed! Our courses are designed for beginners as well as those looking to upskill. All you need is passion and dedication — we handle the rest.

How long are the training classes? +

Course duration varies per discipline. Contact us on 066 277 3338 for a full outline of each course, including duration, curriculum, and pricing.

Will I receive a certificate after training? +

Please enquire directly about certification. We take your professional development seriously and want to ensure you have the credentials to support your career in beauty.
